Guobao Xiao  肖国宝

    Professor

    Minjiang University, Fuzhou, China

    gbx~~mju.edu.cn

He is a Full Professor with Minjiang University in China from 2018.

In 2016, I received my Ph.D. from Xiamen University (厦门大学), advised by Prof. Hanzi Wang (王菡子 教授).

From 2016 to 2018, I was a postdoctor, working with Prof. Hanzi Wang

My research interest includes Computer Vision.

博士，博士后，“闽都学者”特聘教授，教授，福州大学、福建农林大学、福建师范大学、温州大学外聘硕士生导师。福建省高层次人才（C类）。校学术委员会委员。人工智能系系主任。
