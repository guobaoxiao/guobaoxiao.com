Guobao Xiao  肖国宝

    Professor

    Minjiang University, Fuzhou, China

    gbx~~mju.edu.cn

He is a Full Professor with Minjiang University in China from 2018.

He received the Ph.D. degree from Xiamen University (厦门大学) in 2016, advised by Prof. Hanzi Wang (王菡子 教授).

From 2016 to 2018, He was a postdoctor, working with Prof. Hanzi Wang.

His research interest includes Computer Vision.

博士，博士后，“闽都学者”特聘教授，教授，福州大学、福建农林大学、福建师范大学、温州大学外聘硕士生导师。福建省高层次人才（C类）。校学术委员会委员。人工智能系系主任。
