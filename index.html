Guobao Xiao  肖国宝

    Professor

    Tongji University, Shanghai, China

    x-gb~~163.com

He will join Tongji University (同济大学).

He is IEEE Senior Member, and he received the Ph.D. degree from Xiamen University (厦门大学) in 2016, advised by Prof. Hanzi Wang.

From 2016 to 2018, he was a postdoctor.

His research interest includes Computer Vision.
